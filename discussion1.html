Rho Calc

\(P,Q ::= 0 | for(y \leftarrow x)P | x!(Q) | (P|Q) | *x\)

\(x,y ::= @P\)

Mathematical Model of Pi Calc Grammar

\(P,Q ::= N | (P|Q) | !P | (\nu x)P\)

\(N,M ::= \pi .P | 0 | M + N\)

\(\pi ::= x(y) | \overline{x} y\)

\(P[X] = N[X] + (P[X], X] x P[X]) + P[X] + (X x P[X])\)

$N[X] = (x P[X]) + 1 + (N[X] x N[X])
